Interests

My biggest, oldest and longest passion is traveling. I should thank my parents for providing me with the opportunity to travel at an early age and to fall in love with it. I have two lifetime goals related to traveling. First, I would have traveled to as many countries as how old I am (now I am almost on the edge - I am 32 years old and I have been in 32 countries.) Second one is to see all the new 7 wonders of the world through life (3 left at this point). My newest interest is programming. I still can’t believe that it embraced me so strongly. I wish to dive into the developing world, but I try not to sink at this point in my life. What a wide world exists behind the screens of our smart devices!! I built this page to learn and practice all that cool things like HTML, CSS and JavaScrip. My survival passion - coffee! I love to go to cafes to have coffee and to observe the surroundings or to learn to program.

Life
